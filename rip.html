Sealed products ranked by expected return on a rip

EV = what the pulled singles would actually net you reselling them — live market less ~20% for fees & shipping, with sub-$2 bulk (commons, most reverses, plain rares) counted as $0 because you can't profitably flip it. ROI = net EV ÷ what the sealed costs. Profit odds = how often a rip clears its price after fees (20,000 simulated rips). This is the honest "rip to make money" math — it's a paid gamble, and the cheapest per-pack product is the least-bad rip.
