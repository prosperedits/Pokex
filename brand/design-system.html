Principles — from the locked plan

Gallery in the dark

Card art is the only saturated thing at rest. UI recedes to gray-violet; brand color appears at moments of meaning (value, selection, heat).

The wheel is the site

One interaction, made luxurious: momentum you can feel, a snap you can trust. Nothing competes with the spin.

Price is light

Market value renders as heat — ember glow marks the chase cards. The minimap is a heatmap before it is a navigator.

Honest data

It is a snapshot, labeled as such, timestamped, sourced. EUR never wears a USD tier.
