FAQ

Questions, answered.

Where do the prices come from?

Five public catalog feeds — TCGdex, Scryfall, Ravensburger, dotGG and TCGCSV — re-priced daily in USD and EUR.

Which games are supported?

Pokémon, Magic: The Gathering, Disney Lorcana and One Piece — with more universes rolling out.

Is Crowns free?

Yes. Free tracks the last three sets of every universe; Premium and Collector unlock every set, alerts and analytics.

How often do prices update?

Daily market snapshots, charted so you can watch a card heat up before it moves.

Can I track my own collection?

Premium adds a full binder with live collection value; Collector adds portfolio analytics, P&L and exports.

Do you sell cards?

No — Crowns is a tracker. We surface the market so you decide when to buy, hold or sell.
